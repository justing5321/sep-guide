Want to know some skills you'll learn in SEP?

Google it papa!

Google is your best friend when you have a question. Anything you can't figure out or need help on; googles got it.

w3schools

Unlike google, w3schools is a website built for coders with a passion to learn new things or need a quick code snippet to see how something works.

Communication

While coding you learn how to communicate people by working on group projects or just simply asking your partner a question.

Typing

This is the most important skill you'll learn in SEP. Everyday you get better and better in typing and it shows when you have to code a whole final project essay.
